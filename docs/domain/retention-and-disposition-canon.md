# Retention and Disposition Canon

Retention in AIX is archival/governance-oriented and policy-driven.

Retention policies may define:
- retention duration
- retention trigger
- storage tier transitions
- immutability
- legal hold compatibility
- disposition workflow

Storage tiers are abstracted as:
- HOT
- COOL
- COLD
- ARCHIVE

AIX never performs automatic permanent physical deletion.

Disposition/destruction requires:
- review
- approval
- audit evidence
- workflow execution
