# AI Canon

AI is not part of the transactional core.
AI is optional and provider-agnostic.
AI assists; humans govern.
AI suggests; humans approve.

Potential AI capabilities:
- OCR
- metadata extraction
- classification suggestion
- semantic search
- summarization
- relation suggestion

AI never modifies metadata automatically.

AI outputs are:
- suggestions
- annotations
- derived artifacts

Tenants configure:
- providers
- credentials
- limits
- enabled capabilities
- models

AI-derived artifacts:
- embeddings
- summaries
- semantic indexes
- suggested metadata
- suggested relations

AI-derived artifacts are removable and regenerable.
